About Us

We at Revolution have seen the damage that climate change is doing to our planet. We believe that changing our diet to a more vegetarian based one is a way of solving this problem. As an added bonus, no animals have to be harmed in this process. So we have decided to use the latest technology to serve our customers with 3D printed meat!

3D Printed Meat

We are using the latest technology to bring you 3D printed meat. Our 3D printers are of the highest quality and produce the finest meats. All without harming a single living organism.

Sustainable

We use sustainable ingredients such as soybeans and wheat to create our meat. We work together with sustainable farms and farmers to make sure you enjoy our eco-friendly food.

Delicious

Our 3D printed meat is indistinguishable from real meat, you will absolutely love what we have to serve you. Don't believe us? Come try it out for yourself!
